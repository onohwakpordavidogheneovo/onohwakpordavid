ABOUT US

We are a multi-generational church, built on the faith of our founding members. Our mission is to see individuals of all ages, races, and economic statuses daily cultivating their relationship with God.

LEADERSHIP

Rev. David and Rebecca Trammell
Senior Pastors
Pastor Trammell and his wife, Rebecca Starr Trammell have pastored the church since 1987,
when our founding pastor, William R. Starr, passed away. He and the former Rebecca Starr
were married in 1974, and then spent the next few years on the evangelistic field. After
pastoring for a number of years in Festus, Missouri, they returned to the evangelistic field.
They returned to Albion and subsequently unanimously elected pastors in 1987. In the fall of
2011, he was elected as the UPCI District Superintendent of Michigan. Interesting facts about
David and Rebecca Trammell: He released his first vocal recording, "Don't Lose the Vision" in
2006, while she has written several books.
Email: revtrammell@gmail.com
Twitter: https://twitter.com/trammelldavid



​Rev. Terron and Farrah Erwin
Pastors
 Terron graduated from Indiana Bible College in 2004 with a BA in Biblical Studies. Terron
grew up in a pastor's home and has actively been in ministry since 2000. Farrah is the
daughter of Pastor and Sis. Trammell. She grew up in Albion and attended Christ Apostolic
Church for 19 years before marrying Terron and moving to Kentucky, where he was serving
as Youth Pastor. Terron and Farrah came to Albion in April of 2007. Since then, their family
has grown to include two incredible boys, Clark and Cole. Interesting facts about Terron and
Farrah: He works as a Director of Technology for a public school, while she serves as the
church secretary.
